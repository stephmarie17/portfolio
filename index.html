About Me

I'm a Northern California-native living in L.A. The first website I ever built was a Sailor Moon fansite in the mid-90's, and I found my way back to web development after working as a librarian for years.

Before making the career switch to developer, I obtained a Master's of Information and Library Science from San Jose State University and a Bachelor of Arts in History from California State University, Sacramento.

I love responsive design, user experiences centered on ease and joy, and the magic of JavaScript.

When not trying to learn as much as I possibly can about coding while still staying sane, you can usually find me drinking coffee, reading as many books as possible, playing with my dog, or swinging around in the air at an aerial arts studio. If I wasn't working to become a developer, I would want to join the circus.
